about us

what makes our coffee special?

Old Town Roastery offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in India. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee, we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee.

We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Safehouse Coffee made by our professionally trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. We look forward to serving you at Old Town Roastery Coffee Shop!
